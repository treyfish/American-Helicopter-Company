002 / Our Story

Driven by passion.
Powered by purpose.

From the racetrack to the runway — American Helicopter Company was built on one man's relentless drive to push boundaries. Marine veteran Matt McSwain has spent his life chasing speed, precision, and performance: building his first car as a kid, wrenching on NASCAR pit crews, and launching ventures across the automotive, motorcycle, and aviation worlds.

When Hurricane Helene devastated Western North Carolina in 2024, Matt co-founded Operation Helo — partnering with Operation Airdrop to deliver lifesaving aid to families cut off from the world. There, he watched Robinson helicopters keep flying when everything else stopped.

That's when it clicked. A personal relationship with Robinson president David Smith — and Robinson's willingness to do whatever it took to keep ships in the fight — convinced Matt he wanted to be part of that commitment. Today, that same passion fuels American Helicopter Company.
